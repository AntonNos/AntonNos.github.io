What

is it?

Bitgifting makes it easy to gift Bitcoins to your friends and family. Bitgifting giftcards are for sale at Primera, in denominations of 10, 25, 50 and 100 euro.
The value of your card is in euros so you always know what you’re giving, regardless of the current exchange rate of the Bitcoin.

How

does it work?

1

simply buy a gift card at Primera, in the value you want

2

scratch the layer on the back of the card to show your code

3

go to the redeeming page to redeem your card

4

fill in your email address and cardcode

5

you’ll receive your Bitcoins*

* on the dashboard page you can see what their current value is, you can transfer them to your personal wallet, or cash out your profit to your bank account.
